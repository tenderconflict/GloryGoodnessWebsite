    Pastor Dean Nguyen

    Mục sư Tấn Nguyễn

    Background: 1. Birthdate: October 10, 1960.
    2. Birthplace: Hue City, Vietnam.
    3. At the end of Vietnam war, April 1975, pastor Nguyen left his family in Hue City and escaped to the South for a long and dangerous trip to Saigon City.
    4. 1982: Bachelor of Science from McNeese State University.
    5. June-Dec. 1982: working at PPG Industries as a process Engineer.
    6. 1983-1989: Working as Process Engineer at International Paper Company, Moss Point, Mississippi.
    7. 1989-1991: Master of Chemical Engineering at Northeastern University, Boston Massachusetts.
    8. 1991-1992: Enrolling in pHD program at Maryland University.
    9. 1992-present: Patent Examiner at Patent Office.

    Christian Faith: 1. 1975 Become a Christian in Guam Island.
    2. 1976-1982: Member of Trinity Baptist Church.
    3. 1981: Summer Missionary, supported by Baptist Student Union.
    4. 1983-1989, 1991-1999 Youth leader and Assistant Pastor for many Vietnamese Christian Churches.
    5. 2017: Faith Landmark Bible Institute Bible Correspondence.
    6. 2018-Present: Pastor of Glory Goodness Church, Falls Church VA
